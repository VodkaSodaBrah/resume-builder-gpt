Information needed to create a professional resume

Personal Information:
• Full Name
• Email Address
• Phone Number
• Home Address (optional)
• Zip Code

Work Experience (Please add your most recent jobs, it is recommended to add at least 3, if
applicable):
• Company Name
• Job Title/Position
• Duration of Employment (years and months)
• Responsibilities/Activities (brief description of tasks and accomplishments)
• Location of the Job (city, state, or country)

Education:
• Highest Level of Education Completed/Present
• School/Institution Name
• Years of Study (start and end year)
• Degree/Diploma Obtained

olunteering Experience (if applicable):
• Organization Name
• Duration of Involvement
• Volunteer Role and Responsibilities

Skills:
• Relevant Certifications
• Key Skills (e.g., computer skills, technical skills, interpersonal skills)
• Languages Spoken and Level of Proficiency

References (optional):
• Contact Information for Professional References (name, job title, company, phone, and email)
• Relationship to You (e.g., manager, supervisor)
